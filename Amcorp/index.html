Our Mission
Using an optimum mix of personnel and professions, AMCORP is dedicated to providing the highest quality management and technical services to government and industry. AMCORP uses multi-talented professionals to provide an array of services at very low cost, which provides value to the customer.

Background
- Applied Management Corporation, founded in August 1988, provides engineering, technical, logistics, acquisition and program management.
- We support program/project managers in procuring and managing their acquisitions from cradle to grave; primarily major weapon systems, weapons and combat systems. We also assist program/project managers in managing their field activities. We assist field activities by ensuring program office objectives are being met.
- We are a Small, Woman Owned company
- AMCORP currently has personel supporting US Navy contract at Washington Navy Yard, NSWC Dahlgren, SPAWAR Systems Center Pacific, NUWC Keyport and NSWC Indian Head.
- AMCORP prides itself in providing BEST VALUE to the customer.
